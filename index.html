Our Story

Born from Smoke & Sea

Claw BBQ Bahrain brings the bold flavours of the American South together with the Gulf’s finest seafood. Our custom-built smokers run all day, infusing brisket, ribs, and chicken with hickory and mesquite, while our crab claws are sourced daily and served dripping in garlic butter.

Set inside the Hilton Bahrain, the restaurant blends industrial chic with dark nautical luxury — exposed brick, soft leather, and a glowing amber bar.
